Our Services

Specialized Physical Therapy Services

Women's Health

Led by Dr. Susannah Azofeifa PT, DPT, our specialized women's health program focuses on treating pregnant and postpartum women, pelvic floor dysfunction, and pregnancy-related pain. We provide personalized care for women through every stage of life, combining evidence-based physical therapy with dry needling techniques. Our approach emphasizes movement as medicine, helping women move with confidence and strength while addressing unique health challenges specific to women's bodies and life transitions.

Sports Medicine & Performance Rehabilitation

Led by Dr. Fabian Azofeifa PT, DPT, our sports rehabilitation program combines his expertise as a certified CrossFit Level 1 Trainer with advanced physical therapy techniques. With a strong athletic background including collegiate soccer and Olympic weightlifting, Dr. Fabian provides specialized care for athletes and CrossFit enthusiasts. We treat sports injuries, post-surgical recovery, and performance optimization using manual therapy, movement analysis, and progressive exercise protocols tailored to each individual's athletic goals and training demands.

Manual Therapy & Pain Management

Our manual therapy and pain management program combines the expertise of both Dr. Fabian and Dr. Susannah Azofeifa, offering over 10 years of combined experience in treating chronic pain conditions. We utilize advanced manual therapy techniques, dry needling, and evidence-based interventions to address the root causes of persistent pain. Our personalized approach includes pain neuroscience education, therapeutic exercises, and long-term management strategies. With Daniela's compassionate patient care coordination, we ensure comprehensive support throughout your pain management journey, treating conditions from chronic back pain to complex musculoskeletal disorders.

About Us

Welcome to Horizon Physical
Therapy & Wellness

Horizon Physical Therapy & Wellness is led by Dr. Fabian and Dr. Susannah Azofeifa, co-founders with over 10 years of combined experience in physical therapy and rehabilitation. Our clinic specializes in orthopedic rehabilitation, women's health, vestibular rehabilitation, and manual therapy, providing personalized care for patients of all ages. Dr. Fabian brings expertise in sports medicine, performance rehabilitation, vestibular rehabilitation, TMJ rehabilitation, and manual therapy techniques, while Dr. Susannah focuses on women's health, general orthopedics, and geriatrics. Our Patient Care Coordinator, Daniela Espaillat, ensures every patient receives compassionate, comprehensive care throughout their recovery journey. We believe in treating the whole person, combining clinical expertise with genuine compassion to help you achieve your wellness goals.
