Dr Fabian Azofeifa PT, DPT

Co-Founder of Horizon Physical Therapy & Wellness

Dr. Fabian Azofeifa PT, DPT was born in Costa Rica and raised in North Augusta, South Carolina, where he attended both middle and high school. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Biology from Augusta University and his Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Brenau University. In addition to being a licensed physical therapist, Fabian is also a certified CrossFit Level 1 Trainer. With a strong athletic background—including playing collegiate soccer and competing in Olympic weightlifting at the University Nationals—Fabian brings a unique perspective to patient care. His treatment approach is tailored to each individual, combining skilled hands-on techniques with a fitness-forward mindset and carefully structured progressive exercises. Outside the clinic, Fabian enjoys pushing his own limits at North Augusta Family Y CrossFit, attending church, exploring local coffee shops, and spending quality time with his wife, Susannah, and their son, Luca.

Dr Susannah Azofeifa PT, DPT

Co-Founder of Horizon Physical Therapy & Wellness

Originally from Kentucky, Dr. Susannah Azofeifa PT, DPT earned a Bachelor of Science from the University of Louisville in 2017 before going on to receive her Doctor of Physical Therapy from Brenau University in 2021. She is passionate about empowering individuals through movement, with a special focus on treating pregnant and postpartum women. She believes in supporting women through every stage of life, helping them move with both confidence and strength. Certified in dry needling, she blends clinical expertise with a personalized, compassionate approach to care. She is dedicated to creating a supportive, healing environment where patients feel seen, heard, and encouraged. Outside of the clinic, you'll find her enjoying time with her family, doing CrossFit, sipping coffee with friends, or soaking up the sun at the beach. She also loves spending time outdoors and staying active in her community. Susannah is thrilled to open the doors to Horizon Physical Therapy & Wellness and is excited to serve the local community with expert care and a heart for wellness.

Professional Info

Dr. Susannah specializes in women's health physical therapy, with extensive experience in treating pregnant and postpartum patients. Her approach combines evidence-based practice with compassionate care, focusing on empowering women through every stage of life.

Daniela Espaillat

Patient Care Coordinator

Daniela Espaillat was born in the Dominican Republic and moved to the United States at the age of nine. She earned her high school diploma in Manhattan, New York, where she spent her childhood. In 2015, Daniela relocated to Augusta, Georgia, and later began her studies at Aiken Technical College, aiming for an Associate Degree in Registered Nursing. Throughout this demanding program, Daniela completed 24 credit hours, which provided her with a strong foundation in the healthcare field. Daniela’s passion for learning has driven her to earn certification in Medical Office Procedures and Administration Fundamentals Specialization through the MedCerts online career training program. Daniela's firsthand experience caring for her sick mother during home hospice demonstrated to her how significant empathy and compassion can be in both her life and the lives of others, providing her with a deeper understanding of the essence of serving others. As the Patient Care Coordinator, Daniela is dedicated to demonstrating a strong work ethic that meets the needs of each patient. Whether it's by giving a warm and inviting greeting, taking calls, scheduling appointments, and much more;resulting in a positive and fulfilling experience for those she serves. During her free time Daniela loves to spend quality time with her husband and daughter. You can find her with her spouse cheering on their 11 year old daughter at school games and performances. You can also find her staying active and healthy by working out at the gym and making nutritious meals for her and her family. Additionally Daniela and her family seek to grow in faith by attending church. Last but not least she enjoys looking for ways to empower and encourage others.
